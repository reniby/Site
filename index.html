Emma Waddell is a tenor saxophonist based in NYC. She is currently studying computer science and music composition at NYU Gallatin. She plays saxophone in various pop, rock, and jazz groups in the city.

She is also interested in algorithmic composition, and recently came out with an album that used computer programs to simulate various ornithological processes as a compositional and improvisational tool. This album, entitled Bird Ring, can be found here. It will also be featured in an exhibition on Governer's Island in May.
