The Wafer House is an artist collective in the form of bimonthly parties featuring instrumentalists, vocalists, poets, artists, photographers, and various other artistic styles. It includes both installations and a sequence of performances. In Spring 2020 it received a grant from NYU Gallatin to pay for four events over the 2020 calendar year, which will be upcoming soon.
